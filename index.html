About Me

I am a Self-Taught Programmer, Graphic Designer, Website Designer, Game Designer, Game Programmer, Video Editor, and Music Producer based in Vernal, Utah.

I am familiar with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, JQuery, C#, Unity, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Premiere Pro, and FL Studio.

Although my professional experiences are few, and my skills may seem a bit dispersed, my ability to focus on a project or skill 100% and learn all aspects as thoroughly and quickly as possible, I believe, is one of my greatest assets. I am always striving to improve my skills and/or learn new ones through critical thinking and tool utilization (such as books, or the internet) in order to create the best experiences I possibly can.
